// 05 — COMMON QUESTIONS

FAQ

Find answers to frequently asked questions about our interior design services, process, and pricing.

What interior design services do you offer?

+

We offer comprehensive interior design services including residential interiors, modular kitchens, living room design, bedroom design, office interiors, space planning, furniture design, and complete home renovation. We handle everything from concept to execution.

How long does a typical interior project take?

+

Timeline varies based on project scope. A modular kitchen typically takes 3-4 weeks, a single room 2-3 weeks, and a complete home interior 6-8 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during consultation and maintain transparent communication throughout.

Do you provide 3D design visualization?

+

Yes, we provide detailed 3D visualizations and renderings of your space before starting any work. This helps you see exactly how your finished space will look and allows for any modifications before execution begins.

What areas do you serve?

+

We are based in Sector 27, Noida and primarily serve Noida, Greater Noida, Delhi NCR, and surrounding areas. We have completed projects across various sectors in Noida and Delhi. Contact us to discuss your location.

How much do interior design services cost?

+

Pricing depends on project scope, materials, and design complexity. We offer customized quotes based on your specific requirements and budget. Contact us for a free consultation and detailed quotation for your project.

Do you offer warranty on your work?

+

Yes, we provide warranty on all our work. The warranty period varies depending on the type of work and materials used. All details are clearly mentioned in the project agreement before starting work.
